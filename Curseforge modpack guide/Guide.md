## Step 4. Adding mods

Click the Install content button. Now you'll see a list of mods, a searchbar, and the option to switch to different tabs like the Resource Packs, Data Packs and Shaders Tab.

Now For example you want to play with shaders than if your modpack uses the fabric modloader you type "Iris" in the searchbar and click on install and "Sodium" and click install.

For Forge you need to search for "Oculus" and "Embeddium" instead of "iris" and "Sodium"
